Grandma's Lemon Meringue Pie


One 9-inch pie
30 Min - Prep time
10 Min - Cook time
40 Min - Total
8 Servings

Ingredients

  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 2 tbsp all-purpose flour
  • 3 tbsp cornstarch
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1 1/2 cups water
  • 2 lemons, juiced and zested
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 4 egg yolks, beaten
  • 4 egg whites
  • 6 tbsp white sugar (for meringue)

Directions

  1. Preheat Oven: Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Make Lemon Filling:
    • Whisk together 1 cup sugar, flour, cornstarch, and salt.
    • Stir in water, lemon juice, and lemon zest.
    • Cook over medium-high heat, stirring frequently, until the mixture comes to a boil.
    • Stir in butter.
    • Gradually whisk 1/2 cup of the hot sugar mixture into the egg yolks.
    • Whisk the egg yolk mixture back into the saucepan.
    • Cook, stirring constantly, until thickened. Pour filling into a baked pastry shell.
  3. Make Meringue:
    • Whip egg whites until foamy.
    • Gradually add sugar and whip until stiff peaks form.
    • Spread meringue over the filling, sealing edges to the crust.
  4. Bake: Bake in the preheated oven for 10 minutes, or until the meringue is golden brown.
  5. Cool: Let the pie cool to room temperature before slicing. Enjoy your cakepie!
